Review

Honestly, this movie is stunning. The visuals, costumes, and world-building are breathtaking. Cynthia Erivo as Elphaba is powerful, her voice gives chills, and Ariana Grande as Glinda is surprisingly good. The chemistry between them feels real and emotional. The film captures the spirit of the musical but adds more depth to the friendship and politics behind Oz. Some parts feel rushed if you’ve seen the stage version, but overall it’s magical, tragic, and inspiring at the same time.
